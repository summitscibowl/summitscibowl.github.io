Intermediate Class Series

The Intermediate class series will prepare students for High School Regionals and Middle School Nationals. No prior experience is needed, but students of all experience levels are encouraged to sign up. Each class is taught by our experienced staff members and they are tailored to target the most common topics in Science Bowl.

Who can take this course? Middle School and High School students.

Cost: Biology, Chemistry, Math, and Physics each cost $500; Earth Science and Space Science each cost $250.
Duration: Each class is 2 hours long. The classes run for 12 weeks.

Registration Deadline: May 20th, 2022.
