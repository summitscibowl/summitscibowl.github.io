We are a group of college students from across North America, looking to prepare students for the National Science Bowl competition. The material chosen for each course has been selected carefully by the alumni, to maximize the efficiency of improvement.

Geoffrey Wu

Geoffrey Wu is the lead instructor for space science and mathematics and placed 2nd overall in the 2021 National Science Bowl. He is an International Olympiad on Astronomy and Astrophysics Gold Medalist and 3-time USA Mathematical Olympiad qualifier, in addition to placing 2nd in the 2017 RAYTHEON Mathcounts National Competition. Geoffrey has also qualified for the USA Physics Olympiad 2 times, including earning a silver medal.
