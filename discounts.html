Discounts

Teams with Multiple Members Signing Up:

  • 2 members: 10% off all courses for both members
  • 3 members: 20% off all courses for all members
  • 4+ members: 30% off all courses for all members

Individuals Signing Up for Multiple Courses:

  • 2 courses: 100 dollars off one of the courses
  • 3+ courses: 200 dollars off one of the courses

You can apply more than one discount. Discounts will be applied after the sign up deadline, and payment information will be sent to everyone who signs up after the discounts have been applied. Individual discounts will be applied after the team discounts are applied.
